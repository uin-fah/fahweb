Alumni

The existence of alumni plays an important role in efforts to improve the quality and existence of the university that graduated them. In addition to being a quality target of a college, alumni are also a medium for evaluating and measuring the success of a college. With their work in devoting knowledge and working professionally in the community, alumni are considered to have a very strategic role. Therefore, strategic efforts in order to pay attention to the existence of these alumni must be made, because the benefits of alumni for universities are very large for the sustainability of the process in the long term in various ways.
