FACULTY ADAB AND HUMANITIES

Devotion

The community service programs of the Faculty of Adab and Humanities have now begun to focus on the integration of humanities and Islamic values. In addition, the community service program also includes social activities, mentoring and the Real Work Lecture program. In 2019 the community service carried out has covered a wider area not only in the neighborhood around the UIN Syarif Hidayatulah Jakarta campus as usual. By collaborating in the implementation of community service with various government agencies, organizations and the private sector. The strategy for implementing community service includes using a partnership approach. The partnership concept used is not only triple helix (collaboration of government, universities and the community) but has gone towards penta helix (collaboration of government, universities, communities, companies / industries and mass media).
